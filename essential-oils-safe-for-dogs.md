## 13 Essential Oil Blends for Dogs

For each recipe, combine 5 drops of each oil for a stock blend. When the recipe calls for a hydrosol, mix 5 drops of each oil into 2 oz of the hydrosol. Use the inhalation method with your small stock blend or use in a diffuser. \**Precautions: Don't use any of these recipes directly on your dog's skin or fur. Never allow your dogs to ingest oils in any way.

| Condition | Essential Oils |
|:-|:-|
| Separation axiety | Neroli, Violet leaf, Vetiver, Lavender, and Rose hydrosol |
| Fear of thunder and fireworks | Frankincense, Rose otto, Hops, and Cornflower hydrosol |
| Fear of vet or groomer | Frankincense, Violet leaf, Linden blossom |
| Past abuse/physical trauma | Frankincense, Lindem blossom, Melissa, Rose otto, Yarrow |
| Anxiety | Frankincense, Violet leaf, Lindem blossom, Roman chamomile, Hemp |
| Allergies and itchiness | German chamomile, Sweet orange, Peppermint, and Yarrow or German chamomile hydrosols |
| Fungal skin infection | Melissa, Lemon, Sandalwood |
| Lick granulomas | Roman chamomile, German chamomile |
| Ear infection | German chamomile, Lemon, Melissa, Sandalwood, Oregano, Thyme thymol |
| Eye infection | German chamomile, Sandalwood, Yarrow, Wild carrot seed, and Cornflower, Elderflower, or Helichrysum italicum hydrosols |
| Urinary/bladder | Juniper berry, Sandalwood, yarrow |
| Diarrhea | Garlic, Clove bud, Frankincense, Oregano, Thyme thymol |
| Spaying/neutering | Sandalwood, German chamomile, Wintergreen
